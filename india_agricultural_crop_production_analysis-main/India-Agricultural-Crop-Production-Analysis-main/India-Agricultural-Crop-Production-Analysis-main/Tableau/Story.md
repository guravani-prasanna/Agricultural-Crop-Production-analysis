# Tableau Story

## Story Point 1 – Introduction
India is one of the world's largest agricultural producers. This project analyzes crop production data to understand production patterns across different states and years.

## Story Point 2 – State-wise Production
This visualization compares crop production among different states and identifies the top-producing states.

## Story Point 3 – Crop Analysis
Different crops contribute differently to India's agricultural output. This section highlights the most significant crops.

## Story Point 4 – Seasonal Analysis
Production is analyzed across different seasons such as Kharif, Rabi, and Whole Year.

## Story Point 5 – Year-wise Trend
This visualization shows how crop production has changed over the years.

## Story Point 6 – Key Insights
- Rice and Wheat are among the major crops.
- Some states consistently contribute more than others.
- Seasonal variations affect production significantly.

## Story Point 7 – Conclusion
The dashboard provides useful insights into agricultural production and helps support better planning and decision-making.
